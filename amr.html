The future of robotics is mobile and autonomous

Thanks to advanced sensor technology, no-code programming and state-of-the-art battery technology, autonomous mobile robotics can navigate, detect obstacles and reroute safely in real time.

All KUKA vehicles use Li-ion batteries with inductive charging mode for stable performance. The result: 24/7 operation and 99% availability across warehousing, manufacturing, healthcare and retail.
